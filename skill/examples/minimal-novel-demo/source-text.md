# Minimal Novel Demo Source

At dawn, Mara Vale crossed the Lantern Causeway while the tide still slept below the stones. Her brass map case knocked against her coat with every step.

Brother Calen waited at the last lantern, gray robes wet with mist. He warned Mara that the Tidemark Archive would open only once before the storm bell rang.

In the archive tower, Lio the apprentice hid a cracked compass beneath a stack of salt-stained charts. The compass pointed not north, but toward whatever name its holder feared most.
